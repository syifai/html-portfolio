Procedure for Organizing a Filing System:

  1. Preparation

    • Identify the categories of documents:

      • Personal Documents

        1. Identification

          • Passport

          • Driver's License

        2. Financial

          • Bank Statements

          • Tax Returns

      • Work Documents

        1. Reports

          • Monthly Reports

          • Annual Reports

        2. Presentations

          • Sales Pitch

          • Project Updates

  1. Sorting

    • Gather all documents into one place.

    • Divide documents into the identified categories.

      • Use color-coded folders:

        1. Red for Personal Documents.

          • Labeled for subcategories (e.g., "Financial").

        2. Blue for Work Documents.

          • Labeled for projects or clients.

  1. Labeling

    • Use a labeling machine for folders.

      • Ensure clear and legible text:

        1. Font size 14 or larger.

        2. Bold font for main categories.

      • Apply labels to the top or side of folders:

        1. Top for stackable storage.

        2. Side for file cabinets.

  1. Storing

    • Place folders in a designated storage space:

      • Filing cabinet.

        1. Lockable for sensitive documents.

        2. Arrange alphabetically or by priority.

      • Desk drawer.

        1. Frequently used files.

        2. Accessible within arm's reach.
